Common Pests

Rats

Rats mark their territory by urinating everywhere they go, representing a significant public health risk.

Mice

As they scurry around, they carry dirt and bacteria, transferring it to your counter tops and cabinets.

Ants

Ants contaminate food, build unsightly mounds on properties and cause damage to wood and other materials for nesting.

Cockroaches

They are known to carry organisms which cause food poisoning in humans and many other bacteria, such as salmonella.

Fleas

Although fleas do not pose a direct health risk, flea bites can cause intense irritation and itching to pets and humans alike.
